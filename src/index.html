Rhode Island School Mobility

Changing schools midyear means disruption in curriculum, expectations, relationships, and more for students. These changes can have long-term impact on student learning.
In this visualization, we'll explore student mobility--defined as students changing schools midyear, between Oct. 1 and June 1--as a relationship between schools and districts. A schools' mobility rate is the sum of entering and exiting students divided by the average number of students enrolled on a given day in the school year. This exploration both displays overall mobility rates and explores the paths of students who enter RI schools midyear.
